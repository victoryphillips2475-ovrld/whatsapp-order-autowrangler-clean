# OrderStream Merchant Portal – Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-23

---

## 1. Introduction

OrderStream Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") operates the OrderStream Merchant Portal (the “Platform”), a cloud‑based order management solution for merchants in Nigeria. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect personal data in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and applicable international privacy standards.

By using the Platform you consent to the practices described herein. If you do not agree, you must discontinue use of the Platform.

## 2. Definitions

- **Personal Data** – any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, including name, email address, phone number, payment details, and customer transaction data.
- **Sensitive Personal Data** – personal data revealing racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health information, or biometric data. (We do not collect such data.)
- **Processing** – any operation performed on personal data, such as collection, storage, alteration, transmission, or erasure.
- **Data Subject** – the individual whose personal data is processed.
- **Controller** – the entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data (OrderStream).
- **Processor** – a third‑party that processes personal data on our behalf (e.g., Paystack, Flutterwave, WhatsApp Business API).

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Business Registration Details** – corporate name, registration number, tax identification number (TIN), and address.
- **Contact Information** – email address, phone number, and WhatsApp Business number.
- **User Credentials** – username, password, and security question answers.
- **Payment Information** – bank account details, card numbers, and transaction IDs, processed via Paystack or Flutterwave.
- **Customer Data (on your behalf)** – names, email addresses, phone numbers, shipping addresses, order details, and communication logs generated through the Platform.

### 3.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information** – IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
- **Log Data** – timestamps of logins, page visits, and API calls.
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies** – as described in the Cookie Policy.

### 3.3 Information from Third‑Party Services
- **Payment Processors** – Paystack and Flutterwave provide transaction status, payment confirmations, and refund data.
- **WhatsApp Business API** – message delivery receipts, timestamps, and phone numbers used for communication.

## 4. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under the NDPR, we rely on the following lawful bases:
- **Contractual Necessity** – processing is required to fulfil the merchant services contract (e.g., order processing, invoicing).
- **Legal Obligation** – compliance with tax, accounting, and anti‑money‑laundering obligations.
- **Legitimate Interests** – improving the Platform, fraud detection, and network security, provided it does not override your rights.
- **Consent** – where we process data for marketing communications or analytics beyond essential operation; you may withdraw consent at any time.

## 5. How We Use Personal Data

1. **Provision of Services** – to create and manage merchant accounts, process orders, generate invoices, and facilitate payments.
2. **Customer Communication** – to send order confirmations, status updates, and support messages via email, SMS, or WhatsApp Business.
3. **Support & Maintenance** – to respond to help‑desk enquiries, debug issues, and improve the Platform.
4. **Analytics & Reporting** – to aggregate usage statistics, monitor performance, and generate internal reports.
5. **Legal & Regulatory Compliance** – to retain records for tax, audit, and NDPR compliance, and to respond to lawful requests.
6. **Marketing (Optional)** – to inform you of new features, updates, or promotional offers, provided you have opted in.

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal data with the following categories of recipients:
- **Payment Processors** – Paystack and Flutterwave for transaction processing.
- **Communications Providers** – WhatsApp Business API for messaging services.
- **Cloud Service Providers** – hosting and storage services situated in the EU or Nigeria, bound by contractual data‑protection clauses.
- **Legal Authorities** – when required by law, court order, or governmental request.
- **Service Providers** – third‑party vendors performing services such as email delivery (e.g., SendGrid), analytics, or backup.

All third parties are required to adhere to NDPR‑equivalent safeguards and may only process data on our instructions.

## 7. International Data Transfers

OrderStream may store data on servers located outside Nigeria (e.g., AWS data centres in the EU). Such transfers are governed by standard contractual clauses approved by the Nigerian Data Protection Commission, ensuring an adequate level of protection.

## 8. Data Retention

- **Merchant Account Data** – retained for as long as the account remains active and for a minimum of seven (7) years after termination to satisfy tax and accounting obligations.
- **Customer Transaction Data** – retained for a minimum of seven (7) years for compliance with the NDPR and Nigerian tax law.
- **Log Files** – retained for ninety (90) days for security monitoring, then deleted or anonymised.
- **Marketing Consent Records** – retained until you withdraw consent.

## 9. Data Subject Rights

Under the NDPR, you have the following rights concerning your personal data:
1. **Right to Access** – request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
2. **Right to Rectify** – request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
3. **Right to Erase** – request deletion of data, subject to legal obligations (e.g., tax records).
4. **Right to Restrict Processing** – request limitation of processing under certain circumstances.
5. **Right to Data Portability** – receive personal data in a structured, commonly used format.
6. **Right to Object** – object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
7. **Right to Complain** – lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **legal@orderstream.ng** with a verifiable request. We will respond within thirty (30) days.

## 10. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES‑256).
- Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning.
- Role‑based access controls and multi‑factor authentication for staff.
- Incident response plan for data breaches, with notification to the NDPC and affected data subjects within 72 hours where required.

## 11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our use of cookies is outlined in the separate **Cookie Policy** (*cookie-policy.md*). Briefly, we use essential cookies for session management and optional analytics cookies for platform performance monitoring.

## 12. Children’s Privacy

The Platform is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone younger than 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will promptly delete the data.

## 13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect regulatory changes or operational updates. We will provide at least thirty (30) days’ notice of material changes via email and a notice on the Platform. Continued use after such notice constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
