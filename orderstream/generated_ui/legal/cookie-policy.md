# OrderStream Merchant Portal – Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-23

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how OrderStream Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on the OrderStream Merchant Portal (the “Platform”). By accessing or using the Platform you consent to the placement and use of cookies as described below.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website’s server. They enable the website to remember information about your visit, such as login status, preferences, and usage patterns. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and web beacons.

## 3. Types of Cookies Used

| Category | Purpose | Examples | Duration |
|----------|---------|----------|----------|
| **Essential Cookies** | Enable core platform functionality, such as authentication, session management, and security. | `session_id`, `csrf_token`, `auth_state` | Session (deleted when browser closes) |
| **Performance Cookies** | Collect anonymous usage data to improve platform performance and stability. | `ga_id` (Google Analytics), `performance_metrics` | Persistent (up to 12 months) |
| **Functional Cookies** | Remember user preferences such as language, timezone, and UI settings. | `locale`, `theme_preference` | Persistent (up to 12 months) |
| **Marketing / Targeting Cookies** | Currently **not used**. Should we integrate marketing analytics in the future, we will obtain explicit consent before placement.

## 4. Third‑Party Cookies

The Platform may embed content or services from third‑party providers (e.g., Paystack, Flutterwave, WhatsApp Business API, Google Analytics). These providers may set their own cookies on your device. We do not control the content or duration of those cookies, and you should review the respective privacy policies of those providers.

## 5. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
- View cookies stored on your device.
- Delete specific cookies or all cookies.
- Block third‑party cookies.
- Block all cookies (note that essential cookies are required for the Platform to function, and disabling them may prevent you from logging in or using core features).

For instructions on managing cookies in popular browsers, see:
- Chrome: `chrome://settings/cookies`
- Firefox: `about:preferences#privacy`
- Edge: `edge://settings/content/cookies`
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.

## 6. Consent

When you first access the Platform, you will be presented with a banner summarising the use of essential and performance cookies. By continuing to use the Platform, you provide implied consent for the use of essential cookies. You may opt‑in or opt‑out of performance cookies via the banner’s settings link.

## 7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

- **Essential Cookies Disabled** – You will be unable to log in, maintain a session, or perform secure actions such as payment processing.
- **Performance Cookies Disabled** – Anonymous analytics data will not be collected, which may affect our ability to monitor and improve platform performance.
- **Functional Cookies Disabled** – Your language and UI preferences will not be remembered across sessions.

## 8. Data Protection and Security

Cookie data is processed in accordance with our **Privacy Policy** and the NDPR. Cookies do not store personal data such as passwords or payment details; they contain only identifiers and session tokens that are encrypted at rest.

## 9. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or regulatory requirements. We will post any revisions on the Platform and update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
