# WhatsApp Order Auto‑Wrangler – Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-18

---

## 1. Refund Window
Requests for refunds must be submitted within **30 days** of the date of purchase.

## 2. Grounds for Refund
- The product does not function as described (e.g., order parsing fails repeatedly).
- Critical technical failure prevents you from using the service at all.
- Incorrect charge from Paystack/M‑Pesa that we can verify and reverse.

## 3. Non‑Refundable Situations
- Change of mind after the service has been used or after you have successfully exported orders.
- Partial use of the service (e.g., you exported data but request a refund for the remaining period).
- Refunds for third‑party payment fees (Paystack/M‑Pesa fees are non‑refundable).

## 4. Refund Process
1. Email **support@woaw.com** with your order ID, purchase date, and reason for the refund.
2. We will acknowledge receipt within **2 business days**.
3. If approved, the refund will be processed via the original payment method (Paystack or M‑Pesa) within **7 business days**.
4. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is completed.

## 5. Gumroad Note
WOAW does **not** sell through Gumroad; all payments are handled directly via Paystack/M‑Pesa. Therefore, Gumroad’s own refund policy does not apply.
