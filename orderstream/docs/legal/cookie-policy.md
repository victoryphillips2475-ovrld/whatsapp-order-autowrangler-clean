# WhatsApp Order Auto‑Wrangler – Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-18

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website to remember information about you.

## 2. Cookies Used by WOAW
| Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Essential session cookie** (`session_id`) | Keeps you logged into the merchant dashboard. | Session (cleared on logout) |
| **Analytics cookie** (`_ga`, optional) | Collects aggregated usage data to help us improve the service. | 2 years |

We do **not** set advertising or third‑party tracking cookies.

## 3. How to Control Cookies
- You can block or delete cookies via your browser settings.
- Disabling the essential session cookie will log you out and prevent access to the dashboard.
- Disabling the optional analytics cookie will stop collection of usage statistics but will not affect core functionality.

## 4. Consent for Analytics Cookies

We obtain your explicit consent before setting the optional analytics cookie. A cookie banner is displayed on your first visit, allowing you to accept or decline the analytics cookie. The analytics cookie is only set after you click “Accept”. You may withdraw your consent at any time via the banner or by deleting the cookie in your browser settings.

## 5. Contact
For any questions about our cookie usage, contact **support@woaw.com**.
