# OrderStream Merchant Portal – Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-23

---

## 1. Scope

These Refund and Cancellation terms (the “Refund Policy”) apply to all subscriptions, service fees, and transaction‑related charges incurred by merchants using the OrderStream Merchant Portal (the “Platform”). By subscribing to or using the Platform, you agree to be bound by this Refund Policy in conjunction with our Terms of Service and Refund Policy section therein.

## 2. Definitions

- **Merchant** – the business entity that registers for and uses the Platform.
- **Subscription** – a recurring payment plan (monthly, quarterly, or annual) that provides access to the Platform’s features.
- **Transaction Fee** – fees charged by third‑party payment processors (Paystack, Flutterwave) for processing payments.
- **Cooling‑Off Period** – a 14‑day period starting from the date of the first successful payment, during which you may cancel for a full refund.
- **Material Non‑Delivery** – a failure by OrderStream to provide the contracted service for more than three (3) consecutive days without reasonable cause.

## 3. General Refund Principles

1. **No Refunds for Used Services** – Fees paid for periods where the Platform was accessed and used are non‑refundable, except as outlined in Sections 4 and 5.
2. **Third‑Party Fees** – Transaction fees levied by Paystack or Flutterwave are non‑refundable because they are collected by the processors directly. OrderStream does not retain any of these amounts.
3. **Currency** – All refunds will be issued in the same currency and method of payment used for the original transaction, unless otherwise agreed.

## 4. Cooling‑Off Refunds

- **Eligibility** – You may request a full refund within the 14‑day Cooling‑Off Period if you have not used any paid features (e.g., created orders, exported data, or integrated API keys).
- **Procedure** – Submit a refund request via email to **legal@orderstream.ng** with the subject line “Cooling‑Off Refund Request” and include:
  - Your merchant account ID.
  - Date of the original payment.
  - Reason for cancellation (optional).
- **Processing Time** – Refunds will be processed within ten (10) business days of receipt of the request. The amount refunded will be the subscription fee only; any transaction fees already deducted by Paystack or Flutterwave are retained by those processors.

## 5. Refunds for Material Non‑Delivery

OrderStream will consider refunds on a case‑by‑case basis if one of the following occurs:

1. **System Outage** – The Platform is unavailable for more than three (3) consecutive days due to a failure under OrderStream’s control (excluding force‑majeure events).
2. **Critical Bug** – A defect that prevents you from performing core functions (order creation, payment reconciliation) and is not resolved within fifteen (15) days after notification.
3. **Data Loss** – Unrecoverable loss of merchant or customer data caused by OrderStream’s negligence.

**Refund Process** – To request a refund for material non‑delivery, email **legal@orderstream.ng** with "Material Non‑Delivery Refund Request" and include:
- Detailed description of the issue.
- Dates and times of the outage/bug.
- Supporting screenshots or logs (if available).

OrderStream will investigate the claim, and if validated, will credit a prorated amount covering the period of non‑delivery. The calculation will be based on the daily subscription rate.

## 6. Cancellation of Subscription

- **At‑End‑of‑Period Cancellation** – You may cancel your subscription at any time by notifying us in writing at **legal@orderstream.ng**. Cancellation becomes effective at the end of the current billing cycle; you will not be charged for subsequent periods.
- **Immediate Cancellation** – You may request immediate termination. In this case, any prepaid fees for the remaining period are non‑refundable, except where a Material Non‑Delivery claim is approved.
- **Account Deactivation** – Upon cancellation, your access to the Platform will be revoked, and all merchant data will be retained for the statutory retention period (seven years). You may request a data export prior to deactivation.

## 7. Refunds for Transaction Failures

If a payment fails due to an error by Paystack, Flutterwave, or your bank, the transaction will not be captured by the Platform, and you will not be billed. Should you be erroneously charged, contact the payment processor directly for a reversal. OrderStream will assist by providing transaction details upon request.

## 8. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes concerning refunds shall be resolved in accordance with the dispute‑resolution clause in the Terms of Service (see *terms-of-service.md*). This includes negotiation, mediation, and arbitration in Lagos, Nigeria.

## 9. Amendments to the Refund Policy

OrderStream reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email and posted on the Platform with a minimum of thirty (30) days’ notice. Continued use after notice constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
